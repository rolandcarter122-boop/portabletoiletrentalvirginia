    Frequently Asked Questions

    What is the average porta potty rental cost per day?

    While we don't bill by the day for most rentals, shorter-term rentals (like for a weekend) are priced as a complete package. Long-term rentals have a monthly rate that breaks down to a low daily cost.

    How many rental toilets near me would I need for a 200-person event?

    For a standard 4-6 hour event, we recommend at least 3-4 standard units, plus one ADA-compliant unit to ensure accessibility. For a porta potty at a wedding, you might consider one luxury trailer with multiple stalls.

    I need a toilet for rent near me in #State quickly for an emergency. Can you help?

    Absolutely. We specialize in rapid deployment for emergencies. Our temporary toilet rental prices for urgent needs are competitive, and we can often deliver units within 24 hours.

    Do you offer any units that include a porta potty with shower?

    Yes, we have specialized units, including restroom trailers with shower facilities, which are perfect for multi-day festivals, disaster relief, or job sites where workers need to decontaminate.

    Can I request a specific delivery or pickup time?

    We will always do our best to accommodate your scheduling needs. While we typically provide a window for delivery and pickup, you can discuss specific time requests with our team, and we will work to meet them whenever possible.
